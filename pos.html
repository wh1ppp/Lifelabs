Post-Concert Procedure

After the concert, we will first guide the audience outside the venue, to ensure their safety.

Once they leave the venue, we begin to count the number of ticket sales.

Next, we keep a percentage of the money, just enough to pay our financial loss in renting the venue, and artists.

Then, the organizing team will discuss what kind of supplies to donate towards Sitio Bebe.

Then, some of us will be tasked to buy said supplies for Sitio Bebe.

After acquiring the supplies, we will get a trusted delivery carrier to transport the items to Sitio Bebe.

Once the supplies safely travel to SItio Bebe, we will communicate the with the Barangay Captain to distribute the supplies to the locals equally

Finally, we will create a report, documenting our fundraising event.
