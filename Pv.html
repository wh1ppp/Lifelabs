Lifelabs Promotional Draft


Content Strategy

- Identify Relevant Topics: Focus on issues affecting Sitio Bebe, such as health, education, and local development.

- Engagement with Target Audience: Come up with content that students and officials in government will remember, showing the involvement each should have in improving the community.

- Showcase Community Initiatives: Show local projects and fundraising events to attract support and participation.

Needs

- Health Awareness: Educate the residents about health-related issues and services available

- Fundraising for Sitio Bebe: Generate financial support for community projects and initiatives.

- Widening Audience Reach: Expand the viewership to include a wide range of people who can contribute to community development.

Chosen Category

Promotional Vlog

Target Audience

- Students

- Government Officials

- Nearby Community Members

- Local Health Organizations

Research

- Analyze Successful Promotional Vlogs: Watch existing promotional vlogs to understand effective strategies and content styles.

- Differentiate Content Types: Identify what makes certain vlogs successful and how they engage their audience effectively.

Addressing Needs

- Health Education: Provide information on health services available in the area and promote local health initiatives.

- Fundraising Campaigns: Develop content that focuses on fundraising events, including goals, timelines, and how viewers can contribute.

- Community Involvement: Encourage local participation by showcasing stories from community members about the impact of support received.
